About Sudoku


Sudoku is a captivating and fun logic-based puzzle that involves placing numbers from 1 to 9
in a 9x9 grid so that each row, column, and 3x3 sub-grid contains each number exactly once.
Originating in France in 1892 and modernized by American architect Howard Garns,
Sudoku has grown in popularity worldwide due to its simple rules and mental benefits.
There are many variants, including Mini Sudoku (4x4, 6x6) and Mega Sudoku (12x12, 16x16),
but the classic 9x9 grid remains the favorite. Whether you're a beginner or an expert,
Sudoku is an excellent way to exercise your ain while having fun. Happy puzzling!
